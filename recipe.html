The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has to be the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread on the cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Now it's ready to be eaten!
